WHY 3 RUNS?
-----------
- Krippendorff's alpha requires ≥2 coders for reliability
- 3 runs provide robust reliability estimates
- Detects inconsistencies even at temperature=0.0
- Cost scales linearly: 2 runs = $33.22, 4 runs = $66.44
